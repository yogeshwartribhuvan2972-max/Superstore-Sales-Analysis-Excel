# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard – Excel

An interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze Superstore sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, categories, and regional trends.

This project demonstrates the practical use of Microsoft Excel for data analysis, dashboard development, data visualization, and business insight generation.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze Superstore sales data and transform raw data into meaningful business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard.

The dashboard provides insights into:

- Overall sales performance
- Total orders
- Total quantity sold
- Total profit
- Top-performing products
- Regional sales performance
- Monthly sales trends
- Category-wise sales distribution
- Yearly order growth
- Top customers

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis

### 1. Top 5 Products

Identifies the five highest-performing products based on sales.

### 2. Region-Wise Sales

Compares sales performance across:

- South
- Central
- East
- West

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend

Analyzes monthly sales performance and helps identify fluctuations and high-performing months.

### 4. Category-Wise Sales

Shows the sales contribution of the following product categories:

- Furniture
- Office Supplies
- Technology

### 5. Yearly Orders

Analyzes order growth from 2014 to 2017.

### 6. Top 5 Customers

Identifies the highest-performing customers based on their sales contribution.

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- The West region generated the highest overall sales performance.

- The Technology category contributed the largest share of total sales.

- Sales and order volume showed significant growth in 2017.

- Office Supplies showed a stable contribution toward overall revenue.

- Higher discounts negatively impacted overall profitability.

- Central and South regions generated comparatively lower sales than the East and West regions.

## 🚀 How to Use the Project

1. Download or clone the repository.

2. Open `superstore.xlsx` using Microsoft Excel.

3. Navigate to the dashboard worksheet.

4. Use the interactive slicers to filter data by Month, Region, Category, and Segment.

5. Analyze sales, profit, orders, products, customers, and regional performance.
